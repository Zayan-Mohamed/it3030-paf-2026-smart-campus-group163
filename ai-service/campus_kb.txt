
    Campus IT Manual
    ---
    Resetting the Main Campus Router:
    1. Locate the router in the Server Room (Bldg 4).
    2. Power down the attached switch first.
    3. Press and hold the red 'RESET' button on the back panel for 15 seconds.
    4. Wait for the status light to blink green before powering the switch back on.
    
    Lab Safety Protocols:
    - Always wear safety goggles when handling chemicals.
    - In case of a chemical spill, use the yellow spill kit located near the exit.
